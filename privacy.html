Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Riser ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID, email address (if you choose to share it), and name.
  • Alarm Data: Your alarm settings, wake-up preferences, and schedule information are stored to provide our service.
  • Task and Habit Data: If you use our task management and habit tracking features, this data is stored to personalize your wake-up experience.
  • Calendar and Reminders: With your permission, we access your calendar and reminders to include relevant information in your wake-up calls.
  • Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notifications to deliver your wake-up calls.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized AI-generated wake-up calls at your scheduled times
  • Sync your alarms and preferences across sessions
  • Generate contextual wake-up messages based on your tasks and schedule
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Process subscription payments through Apple

AI-Generated Content

Riser uses artificial intelligence (Google Gemini) to generate personalized wake-up messages. Your first name, tasks, and preferences are sent to generate these messages. We do not store conversation history or use your data to train AI models.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with industry-standard encryption. Audio files generated for wake-up calls are automatically deleted within 24 hours.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication, data storage, and cloud functions
  • Google Gemini: For AI text generation
  • Fish Audio: For text-to-speech conversion
  • Apple Sign In: For authentication
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke calendar and reminder permissions at any time

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app in Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Riser is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
