We use your data to:

  • Sync your data: Enable access to your alarms and preferences across your devices
  • Improve the service: Analyze usage patterns to optimize features
  • Technical support: Diagnose and resolve reported issues
  • Communications: Send important service-related updates
Artificial Intelligence

Article 5 - Artificial Intelligence Processing

Riser uses third-party AI services to generate your personalized wake-up messages.

5.1 AI Services Used

Service Provider Function Location
Gemini AI Google LLC Text generation USA (standard contractual clauses)
Fish Audio TTS Fish Audio Voice synthesis Variable

5.2 Data Transmitted to AI Services

When generating your wake-up message, we transmit:

  • Your chosen wake-up style (e.g., "gentle", "aggressive")
  • Your preferred language
  • Your first name (if provided)
  • The titles of your tasks and habits for the day
  • The titles and times of your calendar events
  • Weather conditions in your timezone

5.3 Data NOT Transmitted

  • Your email address or Apple ID
  • Your GPS location
  • Full event details (participants, locations, notes)
  • Your payment information
  • Your contacts or personal messages

5.4 Retention by AI Services

Google Gemini: According to Google's policy, API requests are not used to train models and are not retained beyond processing.

Fish Audio: Voice synthesis requests are processed in real-time and not permanently stored.

Some of our processors are located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), particularly in the United States.

7.1 Safeguards in Place

For these transfers, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): Adopted by the European Commission, these clauses contractually govern the protection of your data

Article 8 - Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Sign in with Apple for secure authentication
  • Access control: Access limited to authorized personnel only
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on Firebase (Google Cloud) with security certifications

Despite these measures, no Internet transmission is completely secure. In case of a data breach affecting you, we will notify you within legal timeframes.
